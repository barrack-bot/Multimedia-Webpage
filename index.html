Why I Love Playing the Keyboard

Playing the keyboard is my favorite hobby. It helps me express emotions and connect with music in a unique way. Whether I’m practicing a new piece, playing worship songs, or just improvising for fun, the keyboard brings me peace and joy. I love the versatility of the instrument and how it fits into so many styles of music.
